This terraced house, set within a conservation area, entailed an exhaustive intervention. The project required Craft Design to thoroughly redesign the existing space. The original fragmented layout, scattered with internal visual constrains, was converted into an airy open area thus enhancing the perceived volume of the house. A generous usage of glass panels as the staircase roof and in the side return extension, allows the building to feel open and to truly enjoy light.
